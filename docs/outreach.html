How to use this directory

These are the people and organizations to consult during Phase 2 — indicator validation. Priority cards (tagged) are the experts Petaluma City Councilmember John Shribbs specifically recommended. The rest are Petaluma- or Sonoma County-based orgs that can advise on specific doughnut dimensions. Click any card to expand contact info, what data we'd ask for, and (where included) a draft outreach message you can adapt.

Reusable template: "I'm working with CalDEC and Amelia Eichel on Petaluma's first Doughnut Economics city portrait — part of the General Plan update. We've drafted indicators for [dimension] and would value your input on whether our metric, data point, and target are the right ones. Could we schedule 20 minutes? Happy to share what we've built."
