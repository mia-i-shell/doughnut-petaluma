Why Microgrids Matter for Energy Equity

Centralized grids create single points of failure and price vulnerability. Distributed energy (solar + storage + smart management) enables:

  • Energy autonomy — Generate your own power, reduce utility dependence
  • Resilience — Keep lights on during grid outages (wildfires, PSPS events)
  • Revenue — Earn money through VPP programs ($300-500/yr in CA)
  • Equity — Community solar and PCEF-type programs make clean energy accessible to renters and low-income households
  • Grid stability — Distributed storage reduces peak demand and defers expensive grid upgrades
